Privacy

aspect.calyvent.com · 100% client-side

ASPECT runs entirely inside your browser. Your SVG files are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server. There is nothing for us to see.

01 · No upload, no server

Every operation — stripping metadata, normalizing the viewBox, optimizing paths, and generating JSX, Vue, Tailwind, or data-URI output — is performed locally using the open-source SVGO library executing in your browser. Your vector files do not leave your device.

02 · What we store

ASPECT uses your browser's local storage to keep a short history of your recent optimizations ("proof slips") so you can restore them within a session. This data lives only on your device, is never sent anywhere, and can be erased at any time using the clear control in the tool or by clearing your browser storage.

03 · No accounts, no cookies, no tracking

There are no user accounts, no authentication, and no tracking cookies. We do not run third-party advertising or behavioural analytics on the tool. We cannot identify you.

04 · Third-party code

The application loads its own first-party scripts and a locally vendored copy of SVGO. No external code executes against the contents of your files.

05 · Children

ASPECT is a general-purpose developer utility and is not directed at children under 13.

06 · Changes

If this notice changes, the revised version will be posted here. Because the tool stores nothing about you, changes only affect how the utility behaves on your device going forward.

07 · Contact

ASPECT is built and maintained by Velocity, a Calyvent studio. Questions may be directed there.
